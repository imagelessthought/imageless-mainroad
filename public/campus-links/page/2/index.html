Registrar Training: AD Astra and Courseleaf

The UNT Registrar provides online training or AD Astra, Courseleaf, and other services. This website provides a link to the materaisl for each of the applications provided by their department. The training materials vary by application: some are available as pdfs, while others have full video tutorials. Courseleaf is a curriculum management solution that is used by the University of North Texas. CourseLeaf offers five software modules that integrate with each other and your system of record to cover everything from course inception through course registration.
